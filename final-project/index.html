Behind the Scenes

This is more background information about the main characters from the iconic "Shrek". It will give you history about their biological families, character traits, and maybe even some rumor-spread theories of an upcoming fourth movie.

Shrek

Main character and decides to marry Fiona, and befriends Donkey and Puss.

Fiona

Was locked up in a tower and had a curse to become an ogre after sunset. Shrek and her fell in love.

Puss In Boots

Got hired to get rid of Shrek by the King due to the fairy godmother's orders. But Shrek got him on his side and later became good friends.

Donkey

Almost got sold, but got away and found Shrek. He realized he wasn't so bad after all and stuck with him through his journey.
